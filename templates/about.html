About Us

Al-Rafidain Solar Company is committed to delivering high-quality solar energy solutions to meet the growing demand for renewable power in Iraq and the region. We believe that solar energy is not just an alternative to traditional energy—it is the future that will lead us toward a cleaner and more sustainable world.

Through innovative systems tailored for individuals, businesses, and large-scale industrial projects, we aim to empower communities to achieve energy independence.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of renewable energy solutions, contributing to a greener, more sustainable future. We strive to build communities that rely on clean energy, reduce carbon emissions, and preserve a healthy environment for future generations.

Our Mission

  • Developing innovative solar technologies that meet our clients’ needs.
  • Promoting the adoption of renewable energy across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.
  • Providing cost-effective and reliable solar power solutions.
